Introducing Notifications

May 31st, 2013

This evening I’ve knuckled down and built a notifications system similar to what you may have used on sites like Facebook. I’ll be making lots of use of it throughout the site, though at the moment it’s used primarily for letting you know when someone has commented on your photos.

For more important notifications you will be sent an email about it, however you can disable this if you like:

Let me know if you find any bugs with it, or have any suggestions for how it can be used across the site.
